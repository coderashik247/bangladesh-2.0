Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

national vision focused on transforming the country through digital innovation, smart infrastructure, and inclusive development. It includes expanding broadband access, improving e-governance, and fostering tech entrepreneurship through initiatives like a2i and hi-tech parks.

Sustainability

meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It focuses on balancing environmental protection, economic growth,promoting clean energy and social development

Infrastructure

basic physical and organizational systems needed for a society to function. This includes roads, bridges, electricity, water supply, internet, schools, and hospitals. Good infrastructure supports economic growth, improves quality of life, and connects people and communities

Empowerment

giving people the tools, confidence, and opportunities to make their own choices and take control of their lives. It often involves education, skills development, and access to resources. Empowered individuals can contribute more effectively to their families, communities, and the economy.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
